## Customer Journey Map Metrics

## Key Takeaways

* Code-verifier's unique value proposition is its high accuracy and reliability, which addresses a significant pain point in the market.
* The customer journey map highlights the importance of providing clear documentation, support resources, and onboarding assistance to ease the adoption process.
* Opportunities to delight users include offering regular updates, new features, and success stories to build trust and confidence in the product.
* Expanding usage requires providing training and certification programs, as well as a community forum for users to share knowledge and best practices.
