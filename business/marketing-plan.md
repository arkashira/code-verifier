# Marketing Plan

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning:** "The most accurate AI code-generation tool that ensures correctness through automated testing and verification."

**Alternative Positionings:**
1. "AI-powered code generation with built-in quality assurance."
2. "Precision code generation for developers who demand correctness."
3. "AI code generation with automated testing and verification."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Persona:** Alex, the Senior Developer
   - **Daily Job:** Alex is responsible for writing and reviewing code, ensuring it meets the required standards.
   - **Budget:** $10,000 - $20,000 per year

2. **Persona:** Sarah, the Engineering Manager
   - **Daily Job:** Sarah oversees the development team, ensuring projects are completed on time and within budget.
   - **Budget:** $20,000 - $50,000 per year

3. **Persona:** Michael, the CTO
   - **Daily Job:** Michael is responsible for the overall technical strategy of the company, ensuring the development team has the tools they need to succeed.
   - **Budget:** $50,000 - $100,000 per year

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads:** $500 - $1,000 per week
2. **Tech Conferences and Webinars:** $1,000 - $2,000 per event
3. **Email Marketing:** $200 - $500 per month

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Launch announcement and product overview
- **Week 5-8:** Tutorials and case studies
- **Week 9-12:** Webinars and live demos
- **Week 13-16:** Customer testimonials and success stories
- **Week 17-20:** Product updates and new features
- **Week 21-24:** Community engagement and Q&A sessions

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize marketing materials and launch plan
- **D-0:** Launch announcement and product release
- **D+30:** First customer testimonials and success stories
- **D+90:** Review and adjust marketing strategy based on performance

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** 1,000 - 2,000 users
- **$MRR Target by D+90:** $50,000 - $100,000
